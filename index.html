Draper Virtual Commune or DVC is a Decentralized Autonmous Organization (DAO) developed by Team Blizzard of July 2020 batch. Our aim is to create a virtual space to support and nurture global entrepreneurs, providing a co-living commune by restoring and repopulating gorgeous villages that are being deserted in Italy and are up for grabs at 1 Euro (case1euro.it ) Currently there are 10 such municipalities in Italy with this program available. Restoration of real estate acquired through these programs are expected to jump in value from 1 euro to tens of thousands of Euros. This appreciation will be exploited by tokenizing this asset and launching of the token in secondary market.

All services that the DVC will provide including voting in the DAO, funding other projects, and expanding the current intitative will be carried through the native DVC Token.
