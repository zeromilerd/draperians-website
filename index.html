About Us

In the last 30 years 1 in 4 small Italian communities have become ghost towns. To prevent these communities from extinction a couple of affected municipalities experimented with a cure. They offered the disintegrating homes for €1. The experiment worked, and citizens from all over the world took up on the offer and started populating these communities. Other villages and towns in the country got inspired from the program and adopted similar programs with more flexible terms. Today there are at least 10 municipalities offering €1 homes with up to 5 years to renovate them.

Draper Virtual Commune - DVC is a Decentralized Autonmous Organization (DAO) developed by Team Blizzard of July 2020 batch. Our aim is to create a virtual space to support and nurture global entrepreneurs, providing a co-living commune by restoring and repopulating gorgeous villages and towns in Europe that are being deserted and are up for grabs at 1 Euro to save them from extinction. All services that the DVC will provide including voting in the DAO, funding other projects, and expanding the current initiative will be carried through the native DVC Token.

We are servicing the saviours and heroes of humanity - draper heroes, providing the alumni an opportunity to form a decentralized organization.

Vision


Our long term vision is to accelerate Draper’s dream of 1 million startups by 2030 by nurturing entrepreneurs globally and changing the world one product at a time.

Mission


To create a decentralized autonomous org (DAO) of Draperians Virtual Commune (DVC) and fund the DAO by tokenizing the potential value in distressed asset in crypto markets earning high ROI for DVC token holders.

Goal


To build our first DAO project around tokenizing for profit unique real estate situation in distressed Italian villages & towns that are giving away homes for a symbolic €1.
